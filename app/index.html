Explore Premier Communities

Discover the neighborhoods shaping Las Vegas luxury living.

Summerlin

Master-planned luxury with guard-gated enclaves, Red Rock views, championship golf, and A+ rated schools. Home to The Ridges, The Cliffs, and Skye Canyon.

Henderson & Green Valley

Refined desert living with upscale dining, top-tier schools, and private communities including MacDonald Ranch, Seven Hills, and Green Valley Ranch.

Anthem & Southern Highlands

Premium guard-gated golf communities in the south valley. Anthem Highlands, Anthem Country Club, and Southern Highlands offer estate living with mountain panoramas.

Las Vegas Strip & Downtown

Iconic high-rise penthouses and luxury condos at Turnberry Place, One Queensridge Place, and Waldorf Astoria Residences with unrivaled Strip panoramas.

Centennial Hills & Aliante

North Las Vegas's fastest-growing luxury corridor with new-construction estates, Skye Canyon, and access to Mount Charleston recreation and open desert vistas.

Boulder City & Outskirts

Unique charm near Lake Mead National Recreation Area. Ideal for estate buyers seeking acreage, privacy, and stunning Mojave Desert and lake views.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of the Las Vegas Valley does Donna serve?

Donna serves the entire Las Vegas Valley - Summerlin, Henderson, North Las Vegas, Green Valley, Anthem, Southern Highlands, Centennial Hills, Aliante, Skye Canyon, Boulder City, Spring Valley, Enterprise, Paradise, and all surrounding communities.

Does Donna have access to MLS listings in Las Vegas?

Yes. Donna is a licensed Nevada real estate agent with full access to the Greater Las Vegas Association of REALTORS® MLS, including new construction, resale, and off-market inventory across the Las Vegas Valley.

How quickly can we list a luxury property in Las Vegas?

Most luxury listings can be launched within 7–10 business days after consultation, professional photography, staging, and media production.

Do you work with off-market buyers and sellers?

Yes. Donna supports discreet transactions across the Las Vegas Valley for clients who value privacy and targeted buyer exposure without broad public marketing.

What is the average price of luxury homes in Summerlin and Henderson?

Luxury homes in Summerlin and Henderson typically range from $1M to $10M+. Guard-gated communities like The Ridges, MacDonald Ranch, Seven Hills, and Anthem Highlands command premium valuations in today's market.

Can I schedule a virtual tour or consultation?

Absolutely. In-person private showings and virtual consultations are available for buyers and sellers across the Las Vegas Valley, including out-of-state clients relocating to Henderson, Summerlin, or any surrounding community.
